About Me...

I am a mostly self-taught developer and thanks to my experience in marketing I have a keen interest in user centered design.

I enjoy identifying and solving problems creatively to deliver clean and creative code. I like to break down problems into smaller chunks before even beginning to write any code. I believe how you approach a problem plays an important role in resolving it.

My marketing experience gives me a user's perspective to ensure what I'm building is a great user experience. Computer Science taught me that proper problem definition and design planning lead to fewer mistakes and bugs during coding and testing thereafter. All this allows me to build web applications that are accessible, scalable and reusable.
