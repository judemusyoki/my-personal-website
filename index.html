About Me...

I am a mostly self-taught developer and thanks to my experience in marketing I have a keen interest in user centered design.

I enjoy identifying and solving problems creatively to deliver clean, creative and user-friendly experience. I also like to break down problems into smaller chunks before even beginning to write any code. This way research, design & development can be done with a proper plan in place.

My marketing experience gives me a user's perspective to ensure what I'm building is a great user experience. My Bachelor in Computer Science taught me that proper problem definition and design planning lead to fewer mistakes and errors during coding and testing thereafter. It also taught me that following certain principles ensures I build web applications that are accessible, scalable and reusable.

I have worked extensively in Kenya, India and now you can find me based in Aarhus Denmark.
